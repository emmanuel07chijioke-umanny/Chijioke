My name is Enyeribe Emmanuel Chijioke.

I am a teenage boy of 18 years aspiring to be a web developer.

As a boy, I aimed to become a medical doctor, but chemistry was my weak subject. I discovered I excel in calculation and critical thinking, which became my turning point. I switched from medicine to Software Engineering.

Life is about passion, dreams, and aspirations. What inspired me most is the quest to create new things.

The Enugu State Governor, Barr. Peter Ndubuisi Mbah, at the Enugu Tech Festival said:

We are not to be the consumers of technology but partakers in the building.

My advice to anyone reading this comes from Swami Vivekananda:

Take up one idea. Make that one idea your life—think of it, live on that idea. Let every part of your body be full of that idea and leave every other idea alone. This is the way to success.
